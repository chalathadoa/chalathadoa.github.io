About Me

WHO AM I

Hello! I'm Udkul Roghodan, a fresh graduate in Computer Science with a strong passion for web development. My journey into the tech world began with a deep curiosity about how websites are built, which quickly turned into a passion for creating user-friendly and functional web applications. During my studies and internships, I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as Codeigniter, gaining a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end development.

My project experience includes designing user interfaces during my internship, building a company-profile website, and developing a comprehensive information system for a Muslimah dormitory as my biggest project so far. This dormitory project involved creating a system with five different user roles and implementing QR code for attendance, providing me with a real experience in creating practical, user-friendly solutions. I'm eager to continue growing my skills and take on new challenges in web development.
